    Step-by-Step AI Workflow

    Discover how Manentia’s AI-powered workflow revolutionizes radiology with seamless integration, advanced processing, and precise reporting for enhanced patient care.

    Step 01

    PACS / Modality Integration

    Connect your imaging devices to Manentia's platform. Upload imaging studies directly for seamless data flow into the AI-powered system.

    Step 02

    Data Transfer to DICOM Server

    Safely transfer and store your imaging data on Manentia's secure DICOM server. Ensure compliance while preparing the data for analysis.

    Step 03

    AI-Powered Image Processing

    Leverage AI to process the uploaded studies. Detect key abnormalities, prioritize critical cases, and prepare actionable insights for radiologists.

    Step 04

    AI Overlay Markings

    Review AI-generated overlays highlighting detected abnormalities on imaging studies. These markings assist radiologists in validating findings quickly.

    Step 05

    Expert-Ready Report Generation

    Automatically generate detailed reports based on AI analysis. Customize templates and review insights before sharing with physicians or patients.
